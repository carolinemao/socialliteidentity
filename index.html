Verifying somebody on Ferns takes two simple steps. First, please enter your email in the box below.

Now, send this link to the person whose photo you want to verify.

The person who you have sent this link will now send you a picture of themselves holding up numbers with their fingers via email. Note that the email you receive will be through our own servers, and the person you are communicating with does not have access to your personal email. If the person is holding up the correct numbers, then feel safe to chat!
